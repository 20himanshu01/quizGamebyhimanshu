WELCOME TO QUIZ GAME
Objective

The objective of the game is to transport a man, a tiger, a goat, and a bundle of grass across a river using a boat.

How to Play

The player takes turns moving tokens across the river using the boat. The player can only transport one token at a time. The player cannot leave the tiger alone with the goat or the goat alone with the grass. The player must strategize and plan their moves carefully to successfully transport all tokens to the opposite side of the river.

Strict Rules

1. The player can only transport one token at a time using the boat.
2. The tiger cannot be left alone with the goat, and the goat cannot be left alone with the grass.
3. The player must ensure that all tokens are safely transported to the opposite side of the river without any of the animals being eaten.
4. The time to solve this puzzle is 15 sec
